Why Grow Strawberries

Strawberries are easy to grow even for beginners, they are nutritious and delicous, especially when you harvest your own at home. Strawberries can grow in most climates so long as you plant them where they can take advantage of full sunlight.

Healthy Benefits

Strawberries are fat free and vitiman rich. Strawberries contain antioxidants in the form af ellagic acid and vitiman c which helps prevent cardiovascular problems and other inflammatory conditions. Strawberries are good for bone health and also help prevent and fight infection.

Where to Grow Strawberries

Strawberries are versatile, Strawberries can be grown in your garden, Strawberries can be grown in various pots or hanging baskets, you can also grow strawberries as house plants if you wish and have adequate sunlight in your window.

What Strawberries like to Grow in

The best soil for strawberries is high in organic matter and drains well. Sandy loam soils are ideal for Strawberries. Clay soils are also good for strawberries if they are improved with organic fertaliser.
